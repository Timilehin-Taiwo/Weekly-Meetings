Step 1 — Structure your sheet

Use exactly these column headers in Row 1:

ATypeTASK or METRIC
BProjectCrossings or Annexe

DResult / ValueBillboard or ₦1.72B
EActionSend renders to MCC by Apr 27

GDueApr 27
HStatusontrack / delayed / update / achieved
METRIC rows use only cols A–D. Example: METRIC | Crossings | cx-inflow | ₦1.72B

Step 2 — Publish as CSV

In Google Sheets: File → Share → Publish to web → select your sheet tab → CSV → Publish. Copy the URL it gives you.

Step 3 — Paste the URL below

The URL looks like: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/e/…/pub?output=csv

ℹ The sheet must be published publicly. Your data stays in your browser — nothing is sent to any server.
